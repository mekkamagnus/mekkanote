Understanding Zettelkasten

Jan 15, 2026 ‚Ä¢ Edited Jan 18, 2026

The Zettelkasten method is a system of note-taking and knowledge management that emphasizes creating connections between ideas.

Key Principles

  • Atomicity: Each note contains one idea
  • Autonomy: Notes are standalone
  • Connectivity: Link notes to create webs

Why It Works

By creating a network of interconnected ideas, Zettelkasten mimics how the brain stores information. This leads to emergent insights and creative connections.
